Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student in Decision, Information, and Communications Engineering(DICE) of Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, The University of Texas at Austin, currently collaborating with Prof. Ahmed Tewfik. I got B.Eng. degree from School of Electrical Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017.

I have broad research interests in Machine Learning and Brain-Computer Interface, whether it be in their development, inference, or more generally their foundations for computational and statistical analysis.

Selected Projects

  • Emotion Recognition using Electroencephalograph(EEG)
    1. Proposed an end-to-end encoder with attention mechanism to decode the EEG signals to different emotion states.
    2. Compared with the other state-of-the-art EEG emotion recognition models on two public EEG emotion datasets SEED and SEED-IV.
    3. The proposed model achieved better accuracy(97.79\% ) and standard deviation(4.89\%) than accuracy(95.44\%) and standard deviation(5.48\%) of state-of-the-art EEG emotion recognition model.

  • Speech Recognition using EEG
    1. Collected EEG data from subjects when they spoke specific sentences to build a continuous-audio-EEG database.
    2. Decoded the EEG signals to the speech texts by applying the state-of-the-art ASR model and proposed a novel EEG signals adapted neural network model.
    3. The proposed model achieved better WER(74.7\%) and CER(76.5\%) than WER(67.4\%) and CER(68.3\%) of state-of-the-art ASR models.

  • Classify Human Smart Phone Interaction Events Using EEG
    1. Collected EEG data from subjects when they operated smart phones to build a EEG-based human smart phone interaction database.
    2. Proposed a GRU-based deep learning model which can learn the right set of EEG features and classify various human smart phone interaction events using only EEG features.
    3. Classified 4 human smart phone interaction events with 90\% accuracy.

  • Face detection and recognition based on surveillance video
    1. Collected related data by using digital image process methods such as smoothing and finding traits based on OpenCV.
    2. Trained a CNN-based deep learning model that can learn the features of faces and can predict and calculate the ration of the area of mouth between the area of face.
    3. Applied the pre-trained model to detect drivers' faces to judge whether the driver is yawning or not.
