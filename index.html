Biography

I'm a M.S. student in Decision, Information, and Communications Engineering(DICE) of Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Texas at Austin, currently collaborating with Prof. Matt Lease at Information Retrieval & Crowdsourcing Lab. I got B.Eng. degree from School of Electrical Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017.

I have broad research interests in machine learning and crowdsourcing & human computation, whether it be in their development, inference, or more generally their foundations for computational and statistical analysis.
