About Me

I am a Senior Applied Scientist at Microsoft, working on agentic LLM systems, evaluation frameworks, and inference pipelines for knowledge-intensive applications.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin in 2023, advised by Prof. Atlas Wang, Prof. Ying Ding, and Prof. Ahmed Tewfik. I received my B.Eng. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017.

My research spans Large Language Models, Agentic AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, and Graph Neural Networks, with a focus on reliability, epistemic integrity, and scalable inference.
