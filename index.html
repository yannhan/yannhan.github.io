Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student in Decision, Information, and Communications Engineering(DICE) of Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, The University of Texas at Austin, currently collaborating with Prof. Ahmed Tewfik, Prof. Ying Ding and Prof. Atlas Wang. I got B.Eng. degree from School of Electrical Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017.

I have broad research interests in Deep Learning , Knowledge Graph, and AI in health, whether it be in their development, inference, or more generally their foundations for computational and statistical analysis.
